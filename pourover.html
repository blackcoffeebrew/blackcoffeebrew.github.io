Pour Over



What You'll Need
Coffee V60 Dripper V60 Coffee Filter Grinder Kettle Scale Timer

Brew Time
  • 3 minutes

Ratio
  • 1:15 coffee-to-water ratio
  • 25 grams coffee, 375 grams water (~4 cups of coffee)
Step 1: Heat Water

Bring more than enough water to fill the French press up to 90℃ (195℉). Later, you can play with the water temperature slightly to find your perfect cup!

Step 2: Grind Beans

Grind your coffee to a coarse, even grind (we use setting #14 on the Baratza Encore). For ~4 cups of coffee you'll want ~25 grams of beans.

Step 3: Place filter in dripper

We recommend wetting the filter with hot water and then dumping the water before proceeding with brewing.

Step 4: Add the ground coffee

Pour the ground coffee into the filter and place brewer on top of a carafe or cup. Place all of this onto a scale and set it to zero.

Step 5: Initial pour and bloom

Start the timer. Begin pouring water slowly over coffee in circular motion starting at the outer rim. Stop pouring when the scale has reached twice the weight of the beans (~50 grams). This pour should take about 15 seconds. Give the coffee an additional 30 seconds to drip while it blooms.

Step 6: Final pour

Add in the remaining water over 1-2 minutes until the scale reaches the required water weight (15x weight of coffee). Take breaks as needed to let the water filter through the coffee grounds.
