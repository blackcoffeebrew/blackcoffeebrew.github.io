French Press



What You'll Need
Coffee French Press Grinder Kettle Scale Timer

Brew Time
  • 4 minutes and 30 seconds

Ratio
  • 1:12 coffee-to-water ratio
  • 30 grams coffee, 360 grams water (~4 cups of coffee)
Step 1: Heat Water

Bring more than enough water to fill the French press up to 90℃ (195℉). Later, you can play with the water temperature slightly to find your perfect cup!

Step 2: Grind Beans

Grind your coffee to a coarse, even grind (we use setting #32 on the Baratza Encore). For ~4 cups of coffee you'll want ~30 grams of beans.

Step 3: Initial pour

Gently pour twice the amount of water than you have coffee onto the grounds. For example, if you have 35 grams of coffee, pour 70 grams of water.

Step 4: Bloom for 30 seconds

Give the grounds a gentile stir and allow the coffee to bloom for 30 seconds. The bloom process releases gasses which have a large impact on the aromas and flavors.

Step 5: Add water and steep for four minutes

Add the rest of the water and place the lid on the grounds. Let the coffee steep for four minutes. Use your timer - don't estimate!

Step 6: Press and enjoy!

Press down the French press plunger until all of the grounds are at the bottom. Serve the coffee immediately. Don't let it sit, as it will over-extract.
